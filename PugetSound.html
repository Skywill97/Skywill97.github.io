Discovery Park is Seattle's largest park with over 8 miles of hiking trails, all of which are open to dogs. The only area of the park closed to four legged friends is the beach.

Loop Trail

The loop trail is a 2.8 mile trail with rolling hills and 140 feet of elevation change. It's perfect for small and large dogs, and has beautiful views of Puget Sound and the Olympic mountains on a clear day.

Beach Trails

The South and North Beach Trails take hikers from the Loop Trail down to the beach and lighthouse. Each trail is about a mile long, with 400 feet of elevation gain. There is a one mile trail that runs along the beach and past the lighthouse, connecting the bottom of the North and South Beach Trails. Add these trails to the loop trail for a 5 mile hike.
